Sherry Yang (杨梦娇)

Email: sherryy at google dot com

 

I am a senior research scientist at Google Brain and a PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Pieter Abbeel. I am interested in deep reinforcement learning and foundation models for decision making.

I received my M.Eng. and B.S. in Computer Science from MIT working with Julian Shun on large-scale high-performance computing for graphs.
